FAQs


1.The CTF competition is online jeopardy style CTF for 48 hours.
2.The competition is hosted on github page https://cursedmonkx86.github.io/aecctf2k19/
3.There are 5 levels (excluding Level 0).
4.Completing each level will lead you to the next one. Winners will be ranked by maximum levels completed.
5.The flag (solution/answer) will be the key for the next level URL. e.g. Suppose, flag for the level 3 is AEC_CTF{h3ll0}, then the URL for the next level (i.e. level 4) will be: https://www.cursedmonkx86.github.io/h3ll0.html .
6.There is a google form after completion of each level. Participants need to submit the flag there with a brief write-up.
7.If the score are the same , the ranking will be determined by the time of last correct submission.
